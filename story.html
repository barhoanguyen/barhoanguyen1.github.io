A VIETNAMESE GIRL'S DREAM

I wanted to create a place here in HCMC where I grew up. Dollhouse Bar is a charming neighbourhood bar, located in the heart of the japan town.

ORIGIN AND TERRITORY

Set in the heart of the city, Dollhouse Bar is a casual, one of a kind bar offering a variety of beers and liquors. We don't have sitting fees or charge extra for drinks. Drink as much as you like, choose one of our beautiful bar hostesses and buy her a drink...or two. Everyone is great company and can speak multiple languages.
